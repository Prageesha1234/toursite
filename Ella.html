Ella

Scenic hill country retreat with stunning views

Discover Ella

Ella is a charming mountain village in Sri Lanka's hill country, surrounded by lush green tea plantations, waterfalls, and misty mountains. Known for its cool climate and stunning scenery, Ella offers hiking opportunities, scenic train rides, and a relaxed atmosphere.

About Ella

Located at an elevation of 1,041 meters, Ella was originally established as a British hill station. Today, it's a popular destination for travelers seeking beautiful landscapes and outdoor activities. The town itself is small with a main street lined with cafes, guesthouses, and shops selling locally made crafts.

Did You Know?

The famous Nine Arch Bridge near Ella was built entirely of solid rocks, bricks, and cement without any steel, during World War I when steel supplies were diverted to war efforts.

Things to Do

  • Hike Little Adam's Peak: Easy trail with panoramic views
  • Visit Nine Arch Bridge: Iconic colonial-era railway bridge
  • Climb Ella Rock: More challenging 3-4 hour hike
  • See Ravana Falls: Beautiful waterfall with legend
  • Tea Factory Tour: Learn about Ceylon tea production
  • Scenic Train Ride: Ella to Kandy or Nuwara Eliya

Best Time to Visit

Ella can be visited year-round, but the best weather is from January to April when skies are clearest. December is particularly beautiful but crowded. May to July sees more rain but lush green landscapes.

Hiking Information

Little Adam's Peak: 1-2 hour easy hike, best at sunrise
Ella Rock: 3-4 hours moderate hike, local guide recommended
Nine Arch Bridge: 30 minute walk from town, best when trains pass (schedule available locally)
What to Bring: Good shoes, water, camera, light jacket

How to Get There

Ella is located in the Badulla District. Access options:

  • By Train: Scenic 7-hour ride from Colombo (recommended)
  • From Kandy: 4-5 hours by train or car
  • From Nuwara Eliya: 2-3 hours by train or car
  • From Colombo: 6-7 hour drive via Ratnapura
